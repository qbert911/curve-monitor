Stable Coin Index Fund Prospectus

Minimum investment is 1k for simplicity sake.

No regular fees, manager takes 10% of profits at time of cash out.

There exist today a variety of digital currencies each pegged to the US Dollar.
There is a large appetite for exchanging between these currencies every hour of every day.
This fund sits in the middle with some of each currency in several different "liquidity pools" and makes income by charging a small fee for each exchange.
By acting as an automated market maker for people wishing to exchange one stable coin for another a relatively safe income stream is formed.


Income is earned in CRV token currency which fluctuates in value between $.50 and $1.00 - the fund seeks to sell its earned CRV when it is at a high, potentially further increasing USD profits.

Risk consideration - if CRV falls precipitously due to a black swan event the profit earned would lose considerable value.

The plus side is that the initial investment funds are not subject to these fluctuations in CRV price at all and safely retain their value.

Investors get access to a website which shows their realtime profits and can cash out at any time.
Cash out is in form of a personal check sent within 10 business days.
